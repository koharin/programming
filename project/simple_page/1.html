IU

She first appeared in Mcountdown, 2008. Her debut title song was ‘Mia’. She was spotlighted by the song, ‘the Good day’. It attracted millions of people in Korea by singing three octaves. From then, she hit ‘You&I’, ‘the Pink shoes’, and so on. In every album, she showed different styles. It is not to say that almost all of her albums were hit.
